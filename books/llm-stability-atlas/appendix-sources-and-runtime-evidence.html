Claim Maturity Summary

Verified

The rerun verified the taxonomy split, Pre-LN as first-line baseline, attention entropy as a precursor, z-loss as a battle-tested default, clipping as necessary but insufficient, and decode-versus-train repetition separation.

Partial

DeepNorm as an escalation is credible but still appendix-limited on coefficient detail; OEC is promising but not yet mature enough to replace z-loss; omni transfer remains an under-sourced inference.

Contradictions And Repairs

  • Pre-LN versus OLMo2 norm placement: resolved by teaching Pre-LN as the historical stability baseline and OLMo2 as a modern variant, not a contradiction-free duplicate.
  • Q/K norm versus soft-capping versus sigmaReparam: resolved by treating them as one family without forcing a universal winner.
  • OEC versus z-loss: resolved by keeping z-loss in the default stack and OEC in the frontier margin.
  • Omni transfer: resolved by leaving it under-sourced and outside the verified claim set.
